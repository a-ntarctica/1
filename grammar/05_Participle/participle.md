# Participle

## Forms table

| Form | Intransitive (Active) | Transitive Active | Transitive Passive |
|------|-----------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Present Participle | going | asking | being asked |
| Past Participle | — | — | asked |
| Perfect Participle | having gone | having asked | having been asked |

## Meanings

| Participle | Meaning | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Present Participle (active) | simultaneous action | Seeing that I was late, I hurried |
| Present Participle (passive) | simultaneous passive | Being written in Spanish, the article was not easy to translate |
| Perfect Participle (active) | preceding action | Having asked a question, the teacher waited for an answer |
| Perfect Participle (passive) | preceding passive | Having been asked, the boy was thinking |
| Past Participle | passive meaning only | A building built many years ago |

## Participial constructions

### Objective case + Present Participle
`pronoun (object case) / noun + Present Participle`
Example: I saw **her writing** the letter.

### Objective case + Past Participle
`pronoun (object case) / noun + Past Participle`
Example: I must have **my watch repaired**.

### Absolute Participial Construction
`noun (common case) + Present/Perfect/Past Participle`
Example: **The article being in Spanish**, I could not understand one word.
