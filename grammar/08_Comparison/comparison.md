# Degrees of Comparison

## Adjectives

| Type | Positive | Comparative | Superlative |
|------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 syllable | new | newer | newest |
| 2 syllables (-er/-ow/-y/-le) | clever, happy, simple | cleverer, happier, simpler | cleverest, happiest, simplest |
| 2+ syllables | beautiful | more beautiful | most beautiful |

### Irregular adjectives

| Positive | Comparative | Superlative |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| good | better | best |
| bad | worse | worst |
| far (distance) | farther | farthest |
| far (time+distance) | further | furthest |
| near (distance) | nearer | nearest |
| near (order) | nearer | next |
| late (time) | later | latest |
| late (order) | later | last |
| old (age) | older | oldest |
| old (family seniority) | elder | eldest |

## Adverbs

| Type | Positive | Comparative | Superlative |
|------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 syllable | hard | harder | hardest |
| Others | beautifully | more beautifully | most beautifully |

### Irregular adverbs

| Positive | Comparative | Superlative |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
