# Prepositions and Service Words

## Preposition types

| Type | Examples |
|------|---------|
| Simple | at, on, in, from, to, with, by, of, for, about, till |
| Derived | into, upon, behind, before, inside, outside, during, throughout |
| Compound | in front of, because of, out of, as far as, in spite of, according to, instead of |

## Key prepositions — at / in / on

| Preposition | Usage | Examples |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| **at** | specific point, time | at 5 o'clock, at night, at school |
| **in** | enclosed space, longer period | in the room, in January, in 2020 |
| **on** | surface, specific day | on the table, on Monday, on 5th May |
