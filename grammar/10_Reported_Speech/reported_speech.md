# Reported Speech (Косвенная речь)

## Tense shifts

| Direct speech | Reported speech |
|---------------|----------------|
| Present Simple | Past Simple |
| Present Continuous | Past Continuous |
| Present Perfect | Past Perfect |
| Past Simple | Past Perfect |
| Present Perfect Continuous | Past Perfect Continuous |
| Future Simple (will/shall) | Future in the Past (would/should) |

## Pronoun and adverb changes

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| this | that |
| these | those |
| here | there |
| now | then |
| today | on that day |
| yesterday | the day before |
| last week | the previous week |
| two days ago | two days before |
| tomorrow | the next day |
| this week | that week |

## Types of reported clauses

| Type | Direct | Connector | Reported |
|------|--------|-----------|---------|
| Statement | "I dance so well" | that | He said (that) I danced so well |
| General question | "Do you like sports?" | if / whether | They asked if we liked sports |
| Special question | "Where did you spend your holidays?" | question word | He asked where I had spent my holidays |
| Command (positive) | "Go to the blackboard" | to + infinitive | The teacher told the pupil to go |
| Command (negative) | "Don't be angry" | not + infinitive | We asked mother not to be angry |
| "Yes" answer | "Yes, I shall do it" | to agree | He agreed to do it |
| "No" answer | "No, I haven't been there" | to deny / refuse | She denied that she had been there |
