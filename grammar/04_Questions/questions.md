# Questions

## General questions (Yes/No)
Structure: `auxiliary verb + subject + rest`

Example: Can you help us? — Yes, I can.

Auxiliaries: am, is, are, was, were, can, could, may, might, should, ought, will, do, does, did

## Special questions

| Question word | Role | Structure | Example |
|---------------|------|-----------|---------|
| What? Who? Which? | direct object | WH + aux + subject + verb | What did you do in school? |
| Why? When? | prepositional object | WH + aux + subject + verb + prep | What are you talking about? |
| Where? How? | adverbial | WH + aux + subject + verb | What house do you live in? |
| How much? Who? (subject) | subject | WH + verb + rest | Who told you that? |

## Tag questions (Разделительные вопросы)

| Main clause | Tag |
|-------------|-----|
| Affirmative | negative tag | He is an engineer, **isn't he?** |
| Negative | positive tag | You didn't go to the theatre, **did you?** |
