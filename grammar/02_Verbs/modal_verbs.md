# Modal verbs

## Overview
`can (could)` · `may (might)` · `must` · `have to` · `be to` · `shall` · `should` · `will` · `ought to` · `need`

## Usage table

| Modal | Meaning | Example | Translation |
|-------|---------|---------|-------------|
| **can** | ability / physical possibility | Can you skate? | Ты умеешь кататься? |
| **can** | possibility in circumstances | You can see the forest through the window | Можете увидеть лес |
| **can** | permission / request | Can I use your car? | Можно взять машину? |
| **can** | doubt | Can it be true? | Неужели это правда? |
| **can't** | impossibility | It can't be true | Не может быть |
| **may** | permission | May I borrow your pen? | Можно взять ручку? |
| **may** | uncertain assumption | He may be ill | Возможно, он болен |
| **might** | reproach / regret | You might have helped me | Ты бы мог помочь |
| **must** | obligation | You must talk to your daughter | Ты должен поговорить |
| **must not** | prohibition | He must not leave his room | Не должен покидать комнату |
| **must** | near-certain assumption | Your father must be eighty | Наверное, ему уже 80 |
| **have to** | necessity (external) | He had to do it | Он должен был сделать |
| **don't have to** | no necessity | You don't have to go | Не обязательно идти |
| **be to** | planned necessity | We are to discuss it next time | Нам нужно обсудить |
| **be to** | orders in reported speech | He says I am to leave you alone | Он сказал, чтобы я оставил |
| **be to** | fate | He was to be my friend | Он стал моим другом |
| **ought to** | advice / desirability | You ought to say a word about yourself | Ты должен сказать |
| **ought to have** | unfulfilled desired action | You ought to have called yesterday | Ты должна была позвонить |
| **oughtn't to have** | undesirable fulfilled action | You oughtn't to have married her | Не должен был жениться |
| **should** | moral duty / advice | It's late. You should go to bed | Тебе нужно ложиться спать |
| **will not** | refusal | The doctor knows I won't be operated on | Не хочу, чтобы оперировали |
| **will** (inanimate) | inability to function | My pen wouldn't write | Ручка не пишет |
| **will** | habitual repeated action | She will sit for hours under the tree | Часами сидит под деревом |
| **need not** | no necessity (present/future) | You needn't be afraid of me | Тебе не нужно бояться |
| **needn't have** | unnecessary completed action | You needn't have hurried | Не нужно было торопиться |
