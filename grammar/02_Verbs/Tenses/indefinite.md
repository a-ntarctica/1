# Indefinite Tenses (Simple Tenses)

## Quick reference

| Tense | Usage | Signal words | Example |
|-------|-------|-------------|---------|
| Present Simple | habitual action, fact, state | every day, always, usually, never | We go to school every day |
| Past Simple | completed action in the past | yesterday, last week, ago | I wrote a letter yesterday |
| Future Simple | action that will happen | tomorrow, next week, soon | Tomorrow I shall go to the cinema |
| Future in the Past | reported speech about future | — | I thought that I should see you |

## Forms: Present Simple

| Subject | Affirmative | Question | Negative |
|---------|-------------|----------|----------|
| I / You / We / They | write | Do I write? | I do not write |
| He / She / It | writes | Does he write? | He does not write |

## Forms: Past Simple

| Subject | Affirmative | Question | Negative |
|---------|-------------|----------|----------|
| All | wrote | Did I write? | I did not write |

## Forms: Future Simple

| Subject | Affirmative | Question | Negative |
|---------|-------------|----------|----------|
| I / We | shall/will write | Shall I write? | I shall not write |
| Others | will write | Will you write? | You will not write |

## Forms: Future in the Past

| Subject | Affirmative | Negative |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| I / We | should/would write | I should not write |
| Others | would write | You would not write |
