# Continuous Tenses

## Quick reference

| Tense | Usage | Example |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Present Continuous | action happening RIGHT NOW | Mother is making dinner |
| Past Continuous | action in progress at a past moment | I was writing when the bell rang |
| Future Continuous | action in progress at a future moment | I shall be waiting for you at 9 o'clock |
| Future Continuous in the Past | reported speech (future in progress) | She thought he would be doing his work |

## Structure: `to be` + verb-**ing**

### Present Continuous

| Subject | Affirmative | Question | Negative |
|---------|-------------|----------|----------|
| I | am writing | Am I writing? | I am not writing |
| You / We / They | are writing | Are you writing? | You are not writing |
| He / She / It | is writing | Is he writing? | He is not writing |

### Past Continuous

| Subject | Affirmative | Question | Negative |
|---------|-------------|----------|----------|
| I / He / She / It | was writing | Was I writing? | I was not writing |
| You / We / They | were writing | Were you writing? | You were not writing |

### Future Continuous

| Subject | Affirmative | Negative |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| I / We | shall/will be writing | shall/will not be writing |
| Others | will be writing | will not be writing |

## Stative verbs — NOT used in Continuous
know, believe, understand, want, love, hate, need, prefer, seem, contain, belong
