# Perfect Continuous Tenses

## Quick reference

| Tense | Usage | Example |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Present Perfect Continuous | started in past, still continuing (or just finished, with visible result) | I have been waiting for you for 4 hours |
| Past Perfect Continuous | was in progress before another past moment | When you came I had already been waiting for 2 hours |
| Future Perfect Continuous | will be in progress up to a future point | By next week you will have been studying here for 4 months |

## Structure: `have/has/had been` + verb-**ing**

### Present Perfect Continuous

| Subject | Affirmative | Question | Negative |
|---------|-------------|----------|----------|
| I / You / We / They | have been living | Have I been living? | I have not been living |
| He / She / It | has been living | Has he been living? | He has not been living |

### Past Perfect Continuous

| Subject | Affirmative | Question | Negative |
|---------|-------------|----------|----------|
| All | had been working | Had I been working? | I had not been working |

### Future Perfect Continuous

| Subject | Affirmative | Negative |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| I / We | shall/will have been doing | shall not have been doing |
| Others | will have been doing | will not have been doing |
