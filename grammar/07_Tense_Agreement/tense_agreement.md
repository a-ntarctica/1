# Sequence of Tenses (Согласование времен)

## Core rule
When the main clause is in a PAST tense, the subordinate clause shifts back.

| Situation | Subordinate tense | Example |
|-----------|------------------|---------|
| Simultaneous action | Past Simple / Past Continuous | I knew you **were ready** |
| Preceding action | Past Perfect | I didn't know that he **had gone** to the cinema |
| Specific time mentioned | Past Simple | I knew you **were in the cinema** yesterday |
| Future action | Future in the Past (would) | I knew that he **would go** to the cinema |
| Condition clause | Past Simple | She said it would be a pity if I **went** into a factory |
| Universal truth | Present Simple (no shift!) | The teacher told us that the names of cardinal points **are** used with the definite article |

## Shift table

| Direct speech tense | Reported speech tense |
|--------------------|----------------------|
| Present Simple | Past Simple |
| Present Continuous | Past Continuous |
| Present Perfect | Past Perfect |
| Past Simple | Past Perfect |
| Future Simple | Future in the Past (would) |
