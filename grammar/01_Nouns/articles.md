# Articles

## Definite article — **the**

| Usage | Example |
|-------|---------|
| Unique objects | the sun, the moon, the earth, the sky |
| Seas, oceans, rivers, deserts, mountains | The Pacific Ocean |
| Ordinal numbers | Our classroom is on **the** first floor |
| Superlatives | This is **the** biggest building in our town |
| Nouns in plural (specific) | **The** apples are on the table |
| Landmarks | The Kremlin |
| Geographical names, museums, newspapers, ships, hotels | The United States of America, The British Museum |
| Cardinal directions | The North, the South, the East, the West |
| Family surnames (whole family) | The Petrovs |
| Previously mentioned nouns | The coat was large, **the** trousers too long |
| With: previous, last, following, next, only, very, same, right | **the** same story |
| Noun representing a whole class | She spoke about the duties of **the** doctor |

### Fixed expressions with **the**
- to take the floor — взять слово
- to tell the time / the truth
- to play the piano / the violin
- to go to the theatre / the cinema
- in the morning / the afternoon / the evening
- in the distance, in the street

## Indefinite article — **a / an**

| Usage | Example |
|-------|---------|
| Profession | My sister is **a** doctor |
| After "there is / was / will be" | There is **a** big pool in our garden |
| Exclamations | What **a** nice evening! |
| After "such" | It was such **a** wonderful day! |
| Time, speed, weight, distance | I'll be in **a** minute |
| Any one item from a group | Take **a** pencil, please |
| Unknown person/thing | **A** man wants to see you |
| Fixed: a good deal, a great deal, a great many | — |

### Fixed expressions with **a**
- on a large scale, as a matter of fact
- to have a look / a smoke / a mind to
- to go for a walk, to be in a hurry
- to be at a loss, to have a headache

## Zero article (no article)

| Usage | Example |
|-------|---------|
| Plural nouns in general sense | These are very good flowers |
| Uncountable nouns in general sense | Life begins early in Moscow |
| breakfast, lunch, dinner, tea, supper | We have lunch at 11 a.m. |
| bed, school, town, table, sea (after prepositions) | to go to bed, to go to school |
| Proper names, days, months | Moscow, Monday, January |
| Family members (within family) | Father! Can you help me? |
